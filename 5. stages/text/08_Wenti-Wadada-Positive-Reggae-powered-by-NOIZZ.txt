Wenti Wadada Positive Reggae Stage powered by NOIZZ is the spiritual heart of the EXIT Festival and the best place to chill with your friends. The famous "meeting point" of EXIT and a hotspot for all lovers of good vibes, relaxed atmosphere and the spirit of Jamaican music, Wenti Wadada Positive Reggae Stage powered by NOIZZ always features a slate of great artists, who offer festival-goers a complete summer experience with unusual and interesting performances of melodic and hot ska, roots reggae, dancehall and reggae music styles.
