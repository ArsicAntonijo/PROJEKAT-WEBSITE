Fusion Stage is one of the biggest stages of the festival. It hosted some of the most prominent rock, pop, and alternative performers, both from Serbia and the rest of the world. But Fusion Stage is also a beehive of musical diversity: whether it's hardcore you crave, r'n'b, hip-hop,old-school punk, world music or brass bands, Fusion Stage has seen and hosted them all! A showcase of fresh names in music and legends of their craft, electrifying performances at this stage are always guaranteed.
