EXIT festival's Main Stage is situated at the very heart of Petrovaradin Fortress, with a capacity for over 35,000 people and a scenic natural backdrop. Every year the Main Stage welcomes renowned stars and artists from all music genres, ranging from rock, pop, hip-hop all the way to house. Along with the most popular performers, it also features the best bass acts in after-headliner sessions, which have become a trademark of the festival, allowing the party to continue until dawn.
